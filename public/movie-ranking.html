My favourite recent movies

These movies have recently left an impact on me

To kill a mockingbird

Simply a fantastic film seeped in the southern gothic genre.
In the modern age it is both a shock and intrigue to see how casual the institutional racism affected public perception and law

The School for Good and Evil

Although this film could easily be written off as fanciful cliché and pre-teen trite, my partner and I found ourselves enamored with it's stylish presentation, themes of women empowerement and it's ability to strike a delicate balance between clichéd fairytale and modern storytelling.

Interview with a vampire

This entry is due to a personal view point and comes at a very particular time. Interview with a vampire showcases a much less high-brow look into the Southern-Gothic genre. It plays on themes of the macabre while keeping a delightfully dark humor throughout it's runtime. With stars like Brad Pitt and Tom Cruise spearheading this homoerotic flick you are sure in for a treat.
